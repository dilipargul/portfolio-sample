DILIP ARUGULA

In today’s complex world, full of distractions and demands on our time, it’s easy to overlook the power of simplicity. We’re conditioned to seek more — more commitments, more material things, more stimulation. But a complex life filled with excess doesn’t lead to more happiness or fulfillment. Often, it’s the simplest things that bring us the most meaning and lift us up when we’re feeling overwhelmed. Think about what restores your sense of wonder or brings you joy. It might be listening to music, reading a book, playing with your dog, or calling a close friend. These small acts are food for our souls but are easy to forget in favor of the next thing on the to-do list or the newest Netflix show (BTW, the new season of Outer Banks is A-MAY-ZING). Don’t underestimate the value of doing less and appreciating the fundamentals that spark inspiration or peace in your life.
